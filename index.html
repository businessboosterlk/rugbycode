Good to know

Questions, answered

Who are Rugby Code's programmes for?

Players and coaches of every age and level, from juniors to senior and premier level. We run player programmes, coach consultancy, 1-to-1 mentoring and touring, in New Zealand and worldwide.

Do you only work in New Zealand?

No. Rugby Code is based in Wellington and works across New Zealand and internationally. We run local and international tours and mentor players and coaches overseas, with contact lines in New Zealand and Ireland.

What does person-first development mean?

We understand the person before the player or coach. Development covers skills and confidence on the field, and life skills off it.

How do I get started, and what does the first chat cost?

Start with a free, no-pressure consult. Tell us about the player, coach, team or club you want to develop and we will map out a simple plan. Message us on WhatsApp or send the enquiry form.

Can parents enquire for junior players?

Yes. Junior development is a core part of what we do, and parents are welcome to get in touch directly on WhatsApp, by email or through the form.

How do I become a Rugby Code coach or ambassador?

Send the enquiry form and choose Becoming a coach / ambassador, or email aaron@rugbycode.co.nz and tell us about yourself.
